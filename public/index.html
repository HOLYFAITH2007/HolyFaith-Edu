About Us

We Shape Your Future

Established in 2007, HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL, Rukadi has grown into a trusted institution committed to providing quality education to the children of Rukadi and surrounding areas. With over 17 years of excellence, we nurture young minds with strong values and a passion for learning.

We emphasize innovative teaching methods, holistic development, and fostering creativity alongside discipline — in collaboration with Mitsure Technologies LLP. Our students consistently bring pride through achievements in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.
