Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2007

Founded in 2007, HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL, Rukadi stands as a symbol of academic excellence, integrity, and holistic development. Established under the visionary leadership of Mr. Madhukar Wayadande and Principal Dr. Nisha Tandale, the school was created with a strong commitment to providing quality education to the children of Rukadi and surrounding communities.

From its humble beginnings to becoming a respected educational institution, our journey has been driven by passion, dedication, and a deep belief in nurturing young minds.

Philosophy

Our Philosophy of Education

At HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL, education goes beyond academics—it is the foundation for a meaningful and successful life.

We recognize that every child is unique. Our approach centers on active learning, encouraging students to question, innovate, and develop critical problem-solving skills for the modern world.

By integrating technology, ethical values, and holistic methodologies, we nurture confident leaders ready to shape a better future.

Milestones That Define Us

Key moments in our journey of educational excellence.

2007

School Founded

HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL was established in Rukadi by Mr. Madhukar Wayadande and Dr. Nisha Tandale with a vision to provide quality English-medium education.

2012

Campus Expansion

New classrooms, science lab, and computer lab added to support growing enrollment and enhance educational infrastructure.

2018

Smart Classrooms

Digital transformation with smart boards, projectors, and technology-enhanced learning tools across all classrooms.

2024
