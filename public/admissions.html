Admissions

Admission Procedure

A simple and transparent step-by-step process to complete your child's admission at HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL.

Step 1: Application Form

Obtain the admission form from the school office or download it from the official school website.

Step 2: Form Submission

  • Fill out the form carefully with all required details.
  • Submit the completed form along with necessary documents.

Step 3: Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Previous School Records (if applicable)

Step 4: Interaction / Assessment

Shortlisted students may be invited for a personal interaction or assessment, depending on the class.

Step 5: Admission Confirmation

Final admission will be confirmed after:

  • Verification of documents
  • Successful completion of fee payment
